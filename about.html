Wheel of Hope

Our event is proudly supported by Wheel of Hope, a non-governmental organization dedicated to empowering and advocating for people who need and use wheelchairs.

The NGO provides a platform that promotes inclusion, accessibility, and equal opportunities in society. Through outreach programs, awareness campaigns, and partnerships, Wheel of Hope is committed to breaking barriers and ensuring that individuals living with mobility challenges are not left behind.

By connecting this mission with the excitement of the Supercar Spectacle, we aim not only to entertain but also to shine a light on the strength, resilience, and limitless potential of people with disabilities.
